A pair of buckets are connected by a *massless* rod. As shown in the figure, the buckets rotate about an axis through the centre of mass of the two-bucket system. If each bucket has a mass $m$ and the rod has length $l$, what is the rotational inertia of the system? Give your answer in terms of $m$ and $l$. Treat the buckets as point masses and recall that $I = \sum\limits_i m_ir_i^2$. Note that it may not be necessary to use every variable.
